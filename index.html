MATARIKI CELEBRATION - FAIR, CONCERT and FIREWORKS
MatarikiCelebration

Bayfield High School is celebrating Matariki. This is our Maori New Year, when the Pleiades constellation rises in the southern sky. FAIR from 3pm CONCERT from 5pm - followed by FIREWORKS on Bayfield Park A variety of performers - from our very own to local primary schools students. As well as community performers such as the Japanese drumming group ‘O-Taiko’. Any profit that is made will benefit our school community by going towards our on-going fundraising efforts to replace the school van. We look forward to you joining us.
